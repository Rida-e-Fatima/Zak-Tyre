Wheel Balancing

Achieve a smooth and comfortable ride with professional wheel balancing services. Wheel balancing is essential for maintaining the proper distribution of weight around each wheel, reducing vibrations and uneven wear. Our skilled technicians use advanced equipment to accurately measure and adjust the balance of your wheels, ensuring optimal performance and safety on the road.

Brake Pads

Ensure optimal braking performance with professional brake pad replacements. Brake pads play a critical role in stopping your vehicle safely and efficiently. Over time, they wear down and require replacement to maintain reliable braking. Our skilled technicians at Prof's Mobile Tyres and Services specialize in brake pad replacements, using high-quality pads that meet or exceed manufacturer standards.

Brake Discs

Maintain reliable braking performance with expert brake disc servicing. Brake discs, also known as rotors, are crucial components that work in conjunction with brake pads to slow down and stop your vehicle. Over time, brake discs can become worn or warped, leading to reduced braking efficiency and potential safety hazards. At Prof's Mobile Tyres and Services, our skilled technicians specialize in brake disc inspections, repairs, and replacements.

Suspension Components

Experience a smooth and comfortable ride with well-maintained suspension components. Suspension plays a crucial role in providing stability, control, and a comfortable driving experience. Over time, suspension components can wear out, leading to a bumpy ride and compromised handling. At Prof's Mobile Tyres and Services, our expert technicians specialize in inspecting and servicing suspension components.

Steering Components

Ensure precise and responsive steering with well-maintained steering components. Steering components, including the steering rack, tie rods, and ball joints, play a vital role in controlling the direction of your vehicle. Over time, these components can wear out, leading to loose or unresponsive steering, vibrations, or unusual noises. At Prof's Mobile Tyres and Services, our skilled technicians specialize in inspecting and servicing steering components.

Car Diagnostics

Modern vehicles are equipped with complex systems and onboard computers that monitor various components and detect potential malfunctions. Car diagnostics involve using advanced diagnostic tools and software to retrieve error codes and analyze data from your vehicle's onboard systems.
